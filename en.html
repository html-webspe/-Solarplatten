FAQ

Solar energy is a clean and renewable source of energy that can help reduce your carbon footprint and reliance on traditional energy sources. By installing solar panels, you can generate your own electricity and potentially save money on your energy bills.
The installation process typically involves a site survey to assess the suitability of your property for solar panels, followed by plan approval and the installation of the panels on your roof or property. Our team will work with you to ensure a smooth and efficient installation process ensuring minimal disruption to your daily routine and coordinate with necessary parties. Once installation is complete, we provide a walkthrough and ongoing support for the system's optimal performance.
The amount you can save on your energy bills with solar panels will depend on a variety of factors, such as your energy usage, the size of your solar panel system, and the amount of sunlight your property receives.
There are a variety of financing options available for solar panel installation in Germany, including direct purchase, KfW loans, and state subsidies. Our team can work with you to help you explore your financing options and choose the best solution for your needs.
Solar panels require very little maintenance, typically only needing occasional cleaning and inspection to ensure optimal performance. Our team can provide guidance on how to properly care for your solar panel system to maximize its lifespan and performance.
Solar panels are built to last and typically have a lifespan of 25-30 years or more. Our solar panel systems come with a warranty to provide added peace of mind and protection for your investment.
